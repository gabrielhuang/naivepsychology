Philosophy

This is a space for me to share my naive and spontaneous thoughts on how humans think, feel and interact with each other. I try to adopt and reconcile the perspectives of a machine learning practitioner, occasional psychonaut, and part-time human â™¥.

One my approaches will be to characterize and simplify various aspects of human behavior and social dynamics using the reinforcement learning framework. Doing that, I hope to simultaneously improve AI and my understanding of humans and the world(s) they have built.

Given infinite time and resources, my dream is to simulate an AI society, which will then build its own AI society and so on and so forth, leading to the AI singularity. I also hope to learn how to steer the world towards a sustainable future.

Note: I will not be rigorous nor cite the literature exhaustively. Many of my ideas will seem as naive or outright wrong (sorry, Reviewer #2). However, I will try to ground every idea either in scientific/casual readings or in my own physical, mental, and spiritual experiences. Also, this is a constant work in progress, so expect broken links and the like.
